The FHPL Journey

While Fatehpuria Hygiene Private Limited (FHPL) was established in 1976 to focus on modern hygiene and e-commerce distribution, we inherit a strong trading and supply chain expertise built over five decades by our parent group.

Today, we are a leading name in the FMCG sector, bridging the gap between world-class brands and the Indian consumer. Our technology-backed fulfillment and priority service levels are built on a partner-first approach.
